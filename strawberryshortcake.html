Strawberry Shortcake Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 package pound cake
  • 3 cups of strawberries, sliced, sweetend
  • 1 (8oz) cool whip

Steps

  1. Cut cake into 14 slices. Place 7 of the slices on individual plates.
  2. Spoon about 3 Tablespoons of strawberries over each cake slice.
  3. Top each with 1/4 cup cool whip.
  4. Repeat

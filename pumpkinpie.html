Pumpkin Pie Recipe

Ingredients

  • 4 oz. cream cheese
  • 1 Tablespoon Milk
  • 1 Tablespoon Sugar
  • 1 1/2 cup Thawed Cool Whip
  • 1 package Graham Crackers
  • 1 cup cold milk
  • 1 16 oz can Pumpkin
  • 2 packages vanilla instant pudding
  • 1 teaspoon cinnamon
  • 1/2 teaspoon ginger
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground cloves
  • 1 stick butter

Steps

  1. Ground graham crackers and add melted butter and sprinkle sugar until desired thickness, form crust and chill.
  2. Mix pumpkin, cold milk, vanilla pudding, cinnamon, ginger, and ground cloves in large bowl.
  3. Put pumpkin mixture ontop of crust and chill.
  4. Mix cream cheese, milk, sugar, and thawed cool whip in large bowl.
  5. Place cream ontop of pumpkin and chill for 4 hours.
  6. Serve.

Fruit Salad Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 can fruit cocktail undrained
  • 1 can pineapple tidbits undrained
  • 1 can mandarin oranges - drained
  • 1 instant pudding - lemon
  • 1 cup whipped cream (cool whip)
  • 1 cup sliced banana

Steps

  1. Mix together all ingredients and chill.
  2. Serve.

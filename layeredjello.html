Layered Jello Recipe

Ingredients

  • 5-10 different flavors Jello
  • Sour Cream
  • Boiling Water
  • Cold Water

Steps

  1. Add 1 box Jello to 1 cup boiling water and wisk.
  2. Split mix evenly into 2 bowls.
  3. To 1st half add 1/4 cup cold water and put in casserole dish, let set in fridge.
  4. To 2nd half add 1/3 cup sour cream and mix until creamy.
  5. Pour second mixture onto 1st layer, let set in fridge.
  6. Repeat

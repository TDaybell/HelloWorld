Mashed Potato Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 pound of potatoes
  • 2 cups of Milk
  • Salt and Pepper to taste
  • 1/2 cup of Sour Cream (optional)
  • 1 stick of butter

Steps

  1. Fill pot with water and 1/2 cup of salt and begin to boil.
  2. Peel potatoes if desired and but into fourths to cook faster.
  3. Boil potatoes for 30 minutes until fork tender.
  4. Drain potatoes
  5. Mash potatoes, butter, milk, sour cream, and salt and pepper until desired thickness
  6. Add desired gravy and serve.
Meat Loaf

Meat Loaf Recipe

Ingredients

  • 2 pound of ground beef
  • 1 large egg
  • Salt and Pepper to taste
  • Garlic (optional)
  • 1 package of crackers
  • 1 bottle of Ketchup

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees
  2. Mash crackers until dust
  3. Mix together all ingredients until firm.
  4. Place meat mixture into glass baking dish.
  5. Spread ketchup onto mixture and cover with tin foil.
  6. Bake for 1 hour.
  7. Let cool and serve.

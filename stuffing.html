Stuffing Recipe

Ingredients

  • 10 loafes White Bread
  • 4-5 medium onions-chopped
  • 3 teaspoons ground sage
  • salt and pepper
  • 3 cups Chicken Broth

Steps

  1. Rip apart bread into bite sized pieces and add to large bowl.
  2. Chop onions and add to bread.
  3. Mix together bread and onions and chicken broth.
  4. Place bread mixture into hot pan with a little bit of oil.
  5. Cook for 45 minutes or until brown and crisp.
  6. Add Ground sage and salt and pepper for taste and mix.
  7. Cook bread mixture in small or medium batches for faster cooking.
  8. Cool and serve.

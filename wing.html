Chicken Wings Recipe

Ingredients

  • 10 chicken wings
  • 3 large eggs
  • Salt and Pepper to taste
  • 1 tsp of Cumin (optional)
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • 4 cups of flour

Steps

  1. Heat fry oil to 350 degrees.
  2. Mix all dry ingredients together.
  3. Wisk eggs in seperate bowl adding salt and pepper.
  4. Dip chicken wing in egg mixture then into flour mixture.
  5. Place chicken wing into oil and cook for 8-10 minutes.
  6. Place chicken wing into bowl and check temperature (chicken wing temperature cooked is 165 degrees).
  7. Add desired sauce to wings and serve.
